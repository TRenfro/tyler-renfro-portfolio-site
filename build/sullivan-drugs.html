Sullivan Drugs

Web Design & Development

Project Purpose

Sullivan Drugs had an outdated website on which the store owners were not able to easily change store information. This caused customers to arrive at store locations during closed hours, resulting in unhappy clients.

The Solution

We decided to build a Content Management System that refreshed the website's design, as well as introduced new helpful features for customers. The new website has accurate store information, links that open directions in Google or Apple Maps, and download banners that link to the prescription refill app, and more.

Final Result

The final result is a custom WordPress theme that capitilaizes on all the needs of the business and customers. Now, Sullivan Gifts have a new website they can update and manage independently.
